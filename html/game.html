Rules of the game

In this game you have to simplify a logic expression as much as you can. You get the expression in the form of a truth table. This table contains all combinations of input that lead to an output true. Your goal is to simplify this in the form of a sum of products. Here an `'` tells you that the variable is false.
Now you can start making your first product term by using the buttons. To add the term to your answer, the Push term button can be used. The remove term can be used in a similar way.
